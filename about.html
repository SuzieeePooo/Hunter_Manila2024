About Us

Passion for Fashion at Affordable Prices

Lee Jin Flores, who is also a part of our group, is the beneficiary of our system. He has a great love of fashion that made him establish his own online business named Hunter Manila.

About Hunter Manila

Hunter Manila is a design thrift online business which started last year, March 20, 2023, here in the Philippines. It specializes in curating high-quality thrifted garments, focusing particularly on jackets, hoodies, and pants, all at accessible price points.

Lee's keen eye for style coupled with his dedication to affordability has cemented Hunter Manila as a go-to destination for fashion enthusiasts seeking both quality and value.
